Senior Secondary Course Grade XII

National Institute of open schoooling–(NIOS) 12th Class /plus two/ Senior Secondary schooling.
We conduct classes/coaching for NIOS -National Institute of Open Schooling in our institute.
Our coaching experts will ensure that students write exams with full confidence.

    Students from QATAR who have passed 10thClass or Secondary Course from any approved Education Board from any country can join to this course. Students who are register immediately after 10th standard, in the same year are required to write NIOS plus 2 exams in
two years’ time. However those students who register after one year of passing 10thclass /Secondary Course can complete their Plus two in 1 year time.

    National Institute of open schooling – NIOS 12th or Senior Secondary certificate is equivalent
to the certificates provided by any other board like CBSE or, ICSE. NIOS Certificate is acceptable
in almost all the universities in India, Pakistan, Srilanka, Bangladesh & Nepal.
